Description

The Telerik UI for ASP.NET MVC ToolBar is designed to hold different types of controls such as buttons, button groups, toggle buttons, split buttons, and other customized elements.

This demo shows how to resize a window with a contact form. The form implements the Send, Discard, Save, and Reply buttons and drop-down lists. To resize the ToolBar, resize the window and, as a result, the buttons will show or hide according to the available space.
