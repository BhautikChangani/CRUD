Description

The Telerik UI for ASP.NET MVC Drawer supports a compact view, that is, a Mini mode. Within the Mini mode, instead of closing, the component changes its width. Most commonly used to maintain quick selection that is available on the side at all times, the Mini mode visualizes only the icons. To enable the Mini mode, use the Mini() option.

This demo shows how to open the Drawer either by clicking on the hamburger icon or by using a swipe gesture on the associated content. The Mini mode can help users navigate immediately and without expanding a side panel.
