Description

Right-to-Left (RTL) support represents the ability of a library, website, or application to handle and respond to users who communicate through right-to-left languages. Right-to-left languages are Arabic, Hebrew, Chinese, Japanese, and others.

Even though the default language input for most users of the web is left-to-right, the RTL functionality bolsters the accessibility of your website, makes it truly global, and supports your localization efforts.

The Telerik UI for ASP.NET MVC TextBox component comes with built-in right-to-left (RTL) support and offers right-to-left rendering.

This demo shows how to set the TextBox RTL functionality by specifying k-rtl in the div class.
